Quick Note

Getting Started

  1. Adding Notes:
    • Type your text in the provided text input area.
    • Click on the "Add" button to save your note.
  2. Deleting Notes:
    • To delete a note, simply click on the note you want to remove from the list.
  3. Note Persistence:
    • Notes will be deleted automatically when you refresh or close the browser.
    • Please note that QuickNote does not store your notes permanently.

Additional Tips:

  • You can add as many notes as you need.
  • Take advantage of the simplicity and speed of QuickNote for quick reminders and thoughts.

Important Reminder:

  • QuickNote relies on browser storage, and notes are stored locally. Make sure to copy important information elsewhere if you need to retain it beyond a browser session.
